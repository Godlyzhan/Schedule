🍳 Meal Prep Guide

Sunday Prep Session (2-3 hours)

🥣 Overnight Oats (7 servings)

Mix in large bowl: 3.5 cups rolled oats, 7 tbsp chia seeds, 7 tbsp hemp seeds

Divide into 7 containers, add to each: 1 cup soy milk, sweetener if desired

Storage: Refrigerate, ready each morning

🍚 Grain Base Prep

Cook large batches:

• 3 cups quinoa (makes ~9 cups cooked)

• 2 cups brown rice (makes ~6 cups cooked)

• 1 cup red lentils (makes ~2.5 cups cooked)

• 1 cup samp (makes ~3 cups cooked)

Storage: Refrigerate in portioned containers, freeze half for later

🥕 Vegetable Prep

Wash and chop: Bell peppers, carrots, cucumber, broccoli, cauliflower

Roast (400°F, 25-30 min): Sweet potatoes, bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms

Storage: Raw veggies in airtight containers, roasted in meal-sized portions

🥜 Snack Prep

Energy Balls: Blend 1 cup dates, 1/2 cup nuts, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, roll into balls

Roasted Chickpeas: Toss drained chickpeas with olive oil, spices, roast 400°F for 30 min

Hummus: Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon, garlic, water

Mid-Week Refresh (Wednesday)

• Prepare fresh salads and smoothie ingredients

• Cook any grains running low

• Prep Thursday-Sunday vegetables

• Make tahini dressing and peanut sauce

Daily Quick Tasks (10-15 minutes)

• Assemble lunch bowls using prepped ingredients

• Start slow cooker meals in morning

• Blend smoothies and smoothie bowls

• Heat and season pre-cooked grains

💪 7-Day Exercise Program

Progressive strength building with bodyweight exercises

Day 1 - Monday

Upper Body Power

Upper Body Strength (60 min)

Warm-up (10 min):
• Arm circles (forward & backward)
• Shoulder rolls
• Light cardio (marching in place)

Main Workout (40 min):
• Push-ups (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
• Pike push-ups (3 sets x 6-10 reps)
• Tricep dips (chair/couch) (3 sets x 8-12 reps)
• Plank variations (3 sets x 30-60 sec)
• Pull-ups/resistance band rows (3 sets x 5-10 reps)
• Rest 60-90 seconds between sets

Cool-down (10 min):
• Upper body stretches
• Deep breathing

Day 2 - Tuesday

Lower Body Foundation

Lower Body Strength (60 min)

Warm-up (10 min):
• Leg swings (front to back, side to side)
• Hip circles
• Light squats

Main Workout (40 min):
• Bodyweight squats (3 sets x 12-15 reps)
• Lunges (3 sets x 10 each leg)
• Single-leg glute bridges (3 sets x 10 each)
• Calf raises (3 sets x 15 reps)
• Wall sit (3 sets x 30-60 sec)
• Step-ups (using chair/step) (3 sets x 10 each leg)

Cool-down (10 min):
• Lower body stretches
• Hamstring and quad stretches

Day 3 - Wednesday

Cardio & Core Blast

Cardio & Core (60 min)

Cardio (30 min):
• Moderate intensity
• Options: jogging, cycling, dancing, YouTube workout videos
• Target: can hold conversation but slightly breathless

Core Circuit (20 min):
• Bicycle crunches (3 sets x 20 reps)
• Russian twists (3 sets x 20 reps)
• Dead bugs (3 sets x 10 each side)
• Mountain climbers (3 sets x 30 sec)
• Side planks (3 sets x 20-30 sec each side)

Cool-down (10 min):
• Yoga flow
• Cat-cow stretches
• Child's pose

Day 4 - Thursday

Full Body Circuit

Full Body Circuit (60 min)

Warm-up (10 min):
• Dynamic stretching
• Light movement

Circuit Training (40 min):
Repeat 3-4 rounds:
• Burpees (30 sec)
• Squats (30 sec)
• Push-ups (30 sec)
• High knees (30 sec)
• Plank (30 sec)
• Jumping jacks (30 sec)
• Rest 60 sec between exercises
• Rest 2 min between rounds

Cool-down (10 min):
• Full body stretching
• Focus on areas that feel tight

Day 5 - Friday

Upper Body Progress

Upper Body Strength (60 min)

Warm-up (10 min):
• Arm circles and shoulder mobility
• Light cardio

Main Workout (40 min):
• Repeat Monday's routine with progression:
• Add 2-3 more reps if possible
• Hold planks 10 seconds longer
• Add resistance band exercises if available
• Incline push-ups (feet elevated)
• Diamond push-ups (tricep focus)

Cool-down (10 min):
• Upper body stretches
• Chest opener stretches

Day 6 - Saturday

Active Recovery

Active Recovery (30-45 min)

Choose one or combine:
• Gentle yoga or stretching session
• Light walk outdoors
• Swimming (if available)
• Mobility work
• Foam rolling or self-massage

Focus on:
• Improving flexibility
• Releasing muscle tension
• Mental relaxation
• Low intensity movement

Day 7 - Sunday

Rest & Prep

Complete Rest (Optional light activity)

Rest Day Activities:
• Complete rest (recommended)
• Light stretching (10-15 min)
• Meditation or breathing exercises
• Plan next week's workouts

Prep for Next Week:
• Review workout progress
• Plan meal prep
• Set weekly fitness goals
• Prepare workout space/equipment

🏋️‍♀️ Exercise Success Tips

📈 Progressive Overload

• Week 1-2: Focus on form and consistency

• Week 3-4: Add 2-3 more reps or hold exercises 10 seconds longer

• Week 5+: Try harder variations or add resistance

🎯 Proper Form

• Quality over quantity always

• Use mirrors to check alignment

• Start with easier variations

• Watch YouTube tutorials for guidance

🗓️ Consistency Tips

• Schedule workouts like appointments

• Start with 3-4 days if 6 feels overwhelming

• Have backup 20-min routines for busy days

• Track progress in a journal or app

🔧 Equipment Alternatives

• Use water bottles as weights

• Backpack with books for added resistance

• Towels for sliding exercises

• Stairs for step-ups and cardio

💧 Recovery & Hydration

• Drink water before, during, and after workouts

• Get 7-9 hours of sleep for muscle recovery

• Listen to your body - rest if overly fatigued

• Eat protein within 2 hours post-workout

📱 Helpful Resources

• YouTube channels: Fitness Blender, Yoga with Adriene

• Apps: Nike Training Club, 7 Minute Workout

• Timer apps for circuit training

• Form check videos for each exercise

🎵 Motivation Boosters

• Create energizing workout playlists

• Find a workout buddy or online community

• Set weekly mini-goals

• Reward yourself for consistency (not food rewards)

🏜️ Johannesburg-Specific Tips

🌡️ Combat Dry Air

• Use a humidifier in bedroom (45-50% humidity ideal)

• Place bowls of water around living spaces

• Drink extra water at altitude

☀️ High Altitude Sun

• UV is 25% stronger at Joburg's altitude

• Reapply sunscreen every 2 hours

• Wear sunglasses and wide-brimmed hats

💧 Extra Hydration

• Apply moisturizer to damp skin

• Use facial mists throughout the day

• Layer hydrating products (toner → serum → moisturizer)

🏠 Indoor Climate

• Limit hot showers (lukewarm is better)

• Use gentle, fragrance-free products

• Consider an air purifier for dust
